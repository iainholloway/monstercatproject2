Pegboard Nerds

Who are the Pegoard Nerds?

The Pegboard Nerds are a Danish and Norwegian EDM duo. Alexander Odden and Michael Parsberg, their name Pegboard Nerds is an anagram of their last names Odden and Parsberg. They've been producing music since the 90's and their song pink cloud (a song for breast cancer research) claimed a top 10 spot on Billboard Dance Albums in October 2015.

Music

The Pegboard Nerds mainly make singles and remixes since their speciality is in being DJ's. In total they have 64 singles and 16 EP's (which are similar to albums). They have 19 apperances on other albums like Monstercat - 8 year anniversary and Codename X. Heart of the Universe is the lastest in their albums (see right).

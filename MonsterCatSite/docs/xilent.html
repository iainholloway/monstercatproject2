who is Xilent?

Eryk Kowalczyk or Xilent is a widely respected dubstep artist exploding onto the music scene in 2011 with his song "Choose Me II" following with "Beyond", and another hit "Boss wave" pushing Xilent to become known for his EDM and dubstep ability. Then in 2015 he released his debut alum We Are Virtual which met support and was well recied with fans and critics alike. In the years following Xilent started streaming on twitch showing his audience skills and techniquences needed to produce high quality content, and while doing this he still made songs but in scarcity. In 2019 Xilent signed onto Monstercat gearing to release another album called We Are Dust, a continuation of the story We Are Virtual set up.

We Are Virtual

We Are Virtual is the first album Xilent released with a recording studio instead of singles. Backed by Audioporn studios We Are Virtual reached the ears of new and old fans alike spreading Xilent as a even more respectable and admireable artist. Each song is crafted to be a different experience and take you on a trip without going anywhere. And so with the drop of We Are Virtual Xilent entered a new phase of his creator career.

We Are Dust

We Are Dust is the second album from Xilent this time from Monstercat. Fans of dubstep and Xilent agree this is one of Monstercats and Xilents best albums out. Praised for its sequencing, sound design, exicution, transitions the list could go on. Fans wait for Xilent to continue on with story carried by both albums and hope to see many more amazing creations in the future.

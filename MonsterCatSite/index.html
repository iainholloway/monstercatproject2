This site is dedicated to promoting 3 creators from Monstercats libary.

Pegboard Nerds

The Pegboard Nerds are a Danish-Norwegian electronic music group, consisting of Alexander Odden and Michael Parsberg. The name Pegboard Nerds is an anagram of the duo's surnames, Odden and Parsberg. Odden and Parsberg first met in 2005 and formed the group in 2011.

Tokyo Machine

Tokyo Machine is a recurring and popular Monstercat artist. He debuted with PARTY, which was released on July 22, 2016 which appears on track 13 Monstercat 28 - Uproar.

Xilent

With a few years of experience and a few million streams, Xilent released his debut album We Are Virtual in May 2015, which was met with critical acclaim.
